ABOUT ME

My name is David W. and I specialize in urban photography. Urban photography can be referred to as the visual imagery inspired by urban scenery in which photographers capture people and or objects in a cityscape. Photography is an avenue that I use to create a visual story for the viewer, artistic expression, and to capture beautiful/ once in a lifetime moments.

David Walker


I possess a keen interest in utilizing photography to explore my creativity and to enjoy the visually appealing city structure. Developing a more in-depth understanding of multiple genres of photography is one of my short-term goals. My long-term goal is to continue to create abstract expressionism, substance and visuals by pursuing an entrepreneurial career in photography. My passion for life and appreciation for its natural beauty inspires me in my craft and gives me the drive to move forward. .
